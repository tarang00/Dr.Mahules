Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our hospital and services.

What are your working hours?

Our hospital is open from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Sundays, we are open from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM for emergency cases and scheduled appointments only.

Do I need to make an appointment before visiting?

While we do accept walk-in patients, we strongly recommend making an appointment beforehand to avoid long waiting times and ensure that you receive the care you need promptly.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Mediclaim, Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, and government schemes like Ayushman Bharat. Please contact our administrative office for more specific information about your insurance coverage.

What should I bring for my first appointment?

For your first visit, please bring a valid ID proof, your insurance card (if applicable), any previous medical records related to your condition, a list of medications you are currently taking, and any recent test results or diagnostic reports.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency medical services for acute conditions related to our specialties. For life-threatening emergencies, we recommend going to the nearest full-service emergency department.

How can I get my test results?

Test results are typically discussed during your follow-up appointment. However, you can also request your results by contacting our reception. For patient privacy and security, results are not provided over the phone unless pre-arranged with your doctor.
